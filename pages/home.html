Questions & Answers

Can I save my information to edit later?

Yes! Your data for each page is saved locally on your computer automatically when you click the save button on that page (on desktop only, no mobile support). If you want to clear your information from your computer at any time, click here.

Do you save/sell the personal information I enter/save?

No way. Nobody but you has access to the information you enter here. It is only saved on your own computer using local storage. That means if you want to come back and edit it later, you must be using the same browser and computer, and don't clear your cache or you'll lose everything.

Can I download my resume as a Word document?

No, not at the moment. We are currently working on figuring out a method to export the resume as a Word document, so for now, only PDF is supported. But remember, you can save your data on the site and come back later to edit it!

You're missing __________  , can I request a feature?

Of course! We are always looking for ideas to make this site better! Please contact us with your suggestions or feedback! We are already working on more features, including LinkedIn integration, more themes, accounts so you can edit your resume from anywhere, and professional resume review.
